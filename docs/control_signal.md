## Bus Output Signals

- CO  - Program Counter out
- RO  - RAM out
- IO  - Instruction Register out
- AO  - Accumulator out
- EO  - ALU out

## Bus Input/Load Signals

- MI  - Memory Address Register in
- RI  - RAM in
- II  - Instruction Register in
- AI  - Accumulator in  
- BI  - B Register in
- OI  - Output Register in

## Control/Operation Signals

- CE  - Program Counter increment
- SU  - ALU subtract
- HLT - Halt clock
